Organizations

For a Strawless Ocean

For A Strawless Ocean is an organization that strives to work with the local community to encourage using alternatives to plastic straws. The organization hopes to push the boundaries on trends in technology, and media but especially in ocean health advocacy.

Our Last Straw

Our Last Straw is a coalition of restaurants, bars, cafes, and venues that are unified in their goal of eliminating single-use plastic straws. The coalition is based in the DC area, and works with local government leaders and the community to acheive their goal.

Lonely Whale

The Lonely Whale organization believes in community and care while prioritizing the community and ocean life. The organization has several initiatives to reduce the amount of toxins in the ocean. The Lonely Whale strives to work with brands and influencers to advise them on how to lead sustainable lifestyles.

Plastic Pollution Coalition

The Plastic Pollution Coaltion is a group of 1,200 organizations in over 75 countries working towards eliminating plastic pollution and its impact on oceans, humans, and the environment. The coalition's vision for the world is to be free of plastic, by eliminating single-use plastic, drive systematic solutions, and enforce regulations.
